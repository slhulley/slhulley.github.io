A BRIEF OVERVIEW

The digital camera is an essential technology that many designers have used in the past and continue to use today. It captures images that can be stored away for inspiration or as ideas for projects. Designers can also take their own photographs using digital cameras to use for websites or other graphics, rather than using stock photo or paying for other online images. The camera has existed for centuries, but today, we are most familiar with the digital camera and digital images. Explained briefly, the digital camera works fairly similarly to the earliest film camera. The camera lens allows light in, while the aperture and shutter speed are altered either manually or automatically (depending on the cameraâ€™s settings) to affect the amount of light being let in. The light reflects off of a mirror and the image is then recorded onto a sensor in the form of digital data or pixels.
