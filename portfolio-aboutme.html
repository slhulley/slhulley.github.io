I am a senior Design student at UC Davis, interested in web and graphic design. As an all-round enthusiast of the arts, I am absorbed by everything “artsy”, including design, visual art, architecture, and music. I’m an avid concertgoer and am obsessed with more bands than I’d like to admit. I am currently interning for Live Nation at a music venue called the Ace of Spades in Sacramento, CA, where I design posters and snapchat filters for sold out shows. Sacramento is on the rise in the art and music scene and it has been very exciting to be part of an up and coming music venue in the heart of downtown.
