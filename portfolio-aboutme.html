I am a senior Design student at UC Davis, interested in web and graphic design. As an all-round enthusiast of the arts, I am absorbed by everything “artsy”, including design, visual art, architecture, and music. I’m an avid concertgoer and am obsessed with more bands than I’d like to admit.
Currently, I am interning for Live Nation at a music venue called Ace of Spades in Sacramento, CA, where I design posters and snapchat filters for sold out shows. Sacramento is on the rise in the art and music scene and it has been very exciting to be part of such an evolving music scene and venue in the heart of downtown Sacramento.
